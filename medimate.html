    • Overview

    Medimate is an IoT-enabled healthcare solution that ensures patients take the right medication at the right time. It combines automated pill dispensing, remote monitoring, and patient history tracking into one device.

    Case Study

    Medication non-adherence is a major challenge in healthcare, leading to worsening conditions and higher hospitalization rates. Our research revealed that elderly patients and those with chronic illnesses often forget doses or take incorrect medications. Medimate addresses this by automating dispensing, sending reminders, and allowing doctors and caregivers to remotely track adherence.

    Hardware Components

      • Microcontroller (ESP32 / Arduino)
      • Stepper Motor for pill dispensing mechanism
      • LCD display for patient instructions
      • Real-Time Clock (RTC) for scheduling
      • Wi-Fi module for cloud connectivity
      • Buzzer and LED indicators
      • Rechargeable battery & charging circuit

    Key Features

    • Automated pill dispensing with audible and visual alerts
    • QR code access to complete patient medical history
    • Low supply detection with refill request alerts
    • Remote patient monitoring for doctors
    • Customizable medication schedules

    Future Scope

    1. Portable and travel-friendly versions
    2. Voice assistance for visually impaired users
    3. Integration with pharmacy delivery systems
    4. AI-based health prediction and reminders

    Applications

    • Hospitals & clinics
    • Elderly care centers
    • Home healthcare for chronic illnesses
    • Post-surgery medication management
