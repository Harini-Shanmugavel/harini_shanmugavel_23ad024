Overview

Indoor air pollution in confined spaces can cause fatal accidents due to toxic gas buildup or oxygen depletion. This project addresses a real-world problem through real-time monitoring of air quality in enclosed environments using Quantum Gas Lidar technology combined with a Data Acquisition (DAQ) system for instant alerts.

Case Study

Incident 1 – Srinagar Family Tragedy

Five family members, including two children aged 8 and 5, died inside a rented accommodation on the outskirts of Srinagar due to toxic fumes from an LPG heater. The cause was asphyxia — lack of oxygen or excess carbon dioxide in the body.

Other Reported Incidents

  • Construction Site Accident: Four men found dead in a water sump at a residential apartment under construction.
  • Storage Room Case: A 12-year-old boy died in a fruit storage room; his mother was rescued in time.

Proposed Solution

  1. Install indoor air quality sensors in high-risk enclosed areas.
  2. If irregular gas concentrations are detected:
    • Quantum Gas Lidar sensor sends a signal to the DAQ system.
    • The DAQ software processes the signal.
    • An instant alert is sent to administrators for immediate action.

Why Quantum Gas Lidar?

  • Accurate quantification of emission rates for multiple gases
  • Remote sensing with pan/tilt-mounted quantum gas sensors
  • UAV & handheld camera integration
  • Continuous 24/7 real-time monitoring, independent of sunlight
  • Large coverage range and non-intrusive detection
  • Reduced cross-sensitivity compared to commercial gas sensors

System Components

  • Quantum Gas Lidar Sensor – Detects and quantifies gas species in real time.
  • Data Acquisition (DAQ) Hardware – Collects data and converts analog signals to digital data.
  • Software Interface (DAQ Software) – Processes incoming data and sends alerts.

Data Flow Mechanism

  1. Detection – Quantum Gas Lidar senses irregular gas levels.
  2. Data Transfer – Sensor sends readings to DAQ hardware.
  3. Processing – DAQ software analyzes data.
  4. Alert – System sends immediate notifications to admin.
